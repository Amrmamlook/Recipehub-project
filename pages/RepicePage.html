Mabrosha With Hibiscus Jam

30 min
EGP9

Dough Ingredients:

  • A cup of butter, oil, ghee, mixture (any fat available)
  • a cup of sugar
  • Vanilla hanging
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 spoon and half baking powder
  • pinch of salt

Jam Ingredients:

  • A bunch of hibiscus
  • hanging starch
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Lemon zest (optional)

Method:

Dough:

  1. Put the cup of butter with sugar, eggs and a spoonful of vanilla in a bowl and melt them
  2. Mix the flour with baking powder and a pinch of salt well
  3. 2 tablespoons of sugar
  4. Pour the dry mixture over the liquid mixture and knead them well (until you get a somewhat stiff dough)

Jam:

  1. Wash the hibiscus with warm water
  2. Put the hibiscus on the fire and leave it until it boils completely
  3. Add sugar
  4. Add (dissolved starch in a cup of water gradually while stirring) Spread 2/3 of the dough in a suitable mold, then add an appropriate amount of jam to it, then grate the rest of the dough on its face and put it in an oven at a temperature of 180 (it is sufficient to be heated if a suitable degree measurement is not available) for about a quarter of an hour or until it is all over Make sure it's ready to be served by its color
